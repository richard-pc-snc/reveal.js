Step 0 -

The Problem

Ms Drouillard is French Teacher. She has between 7 and 10 students in her class. She sets tests every week that are scored out of 30.

Students must acheive a score of at least 10 to pass each test and points are only recorded if the student passes the test.

Students heed to achieve a total of 50 points to pass the course.

She has asked you to make a system that does the following:

  • Holds the names of all students in the class.
  • Allows her to enter a grade for ever student each week.
  • Increase the total points for students who passed the test.
  • Produces a list of students who have complete the course.

Step 1 -

Data Structures

Thinking Points

The first task is to decide on the Data Structures you will need. Can you list out what is needed?
Think about the variables you will need. Can you name these so they match the scenario?
Next what data type will be best? Do you have enough information to get specific about data types? Or do you need to use data types that allow more of a range of information to be entered.
Will each variable hold one piece of information or a set?

Building Points

You will need to hold information on
  • Students Names
  • Test Scores
  • Test Counts
I would make 3 variables
  • studentNames
  • testScores
  • testCounts
All three will be arrays to hold more than one item.
studentNames will need to hold text, testScores and testCount will both hold numbers.
Students can only do whole numbers of tests, they can't take 3.6 tests.
testCount will need to be an integer.
Nothing says students cannot be awarded half marks.
testScores should be a floating point
