Step 0 -

The Problem

Ms Drouillard is French Teacher. She runs short courses that have between 7 and 10 students in each class. She sets tests every week that are scored out of 30.

Students must acheive a score of at least 10 to pass each test and points are only recorded if the student passes the test. Score are always whole numbers.

Students need to achieve a total of 50 points to pass the course.

She has asked you to make a system that does the following:

  • Holds the names of all students in the class.
  • Allows her to enter a grade for ever student each week.
  • Confirms each grade entered.
  • Increases the total points for students who passed the test.
  • Produces a list of students who have complete the course.
