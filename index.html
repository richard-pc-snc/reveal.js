The Problem

Mrs Jones is French Teacher. She has between 7 and 10 students in her class. She sets tests every week that are scored out of 100.

Students must get an average of 50% or more and complete 10 tests to pass the course.

She has asked you to make a system that does the following:

  • Holds the names of all students in the class.
  • Allows her to enter a grade for each student each week or skip students that are absent.
  • Counts how many tests the students have taken.
  • Produces a list of students who have the score required and done enough tests.
